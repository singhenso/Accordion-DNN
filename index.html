New student (first time registering)

  1. Fill out the Accommodation Request Form

    Click the button below to fill out the Accommodation Request Form. Need help? Check out our video:

  2. Submit Documentation of the Disability

    • Documentation of the Disability must be submitted in order to move forward with the accommodation process. Without documentation, it may cause a delay in the student receiving needed accommodations. For a description of accepted forms of Disability Documentation.
    • Documentation of Disability can be submitted using the following methods:
      • Accommodation Request Form
      • Student's Accommodate account

  3. Register for Courses

    • It is necessary for the student to sign up for courses preferably before making an appointment with a Disability Specialist.

  4. Make an Appointment with the Disability Services Office

    • It is necessary for the student to meet with the Disability Specialist, preferably after the student has signed up for courses. It is recommended to do so approximately two weeks before classes begin or within the first two weeks of class. Accommodations are not retroactive so it helps to have them in place sooner rather than later.
    • At this meeting, the Disability Specialist will interview the student and together they will make a plan for accommodating the student's disability.
